Cuidate: Take Care of Yourself

Building Skills to Prevent Pregnancy, HIV, and STD

This survey is about your knowledge, attitude, and behavior related to HIV and reproductive health education. The information you give will be used to improve reproductive health education for people your age.

The answers you give will be kept private so please answer the questions honestly.

Completing the survey is voluntary. If you are not comfortable answering a question, just leave it blank.

The questions that ask about your background (i.e., age and Hispanic ethnicity) will be used only to describe the types of students completing this survey. The information will NOT be used to find out your name.

No names will EVER be reported.

Make sure to read every question carefully. Select the answer or answers that you agree with most.

Thank you very much for your help.
