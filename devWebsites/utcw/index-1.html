Who We Are

Ultra Touch Car Wash has two convenient locations in Treasure Valley, Idaho. Where our customers can receive multiple Auto Services at "One Stop". Which
includes; A Full Service Car Wash, Phillips 66 Gas Station, Convenience Store, Glass Replacement or Repair, and an Auto Detail Shop.

We believe our success is a direct result of our commitment to quality and our fast and friendly
service. The owners do their best to ensure this
by, simply being on site and managing every day operations. So stop by and wash your car.
Meet our managers, staff and owners; you will
be glad you did.

Our History

1998

The owners Steve and Dave started the Meridian location from the ground floor up.

2003

The Overland store location was built.

Their business background started at Albertson's Grocery Stores with a combined management experience of 35 years.
Their experience launched the first Full Service Car Wash and Gas Station in Meridian, Idaho.
Our experienced crews can wash a vehicle in less than 15 - 20 minutes. Our employees greet every customer in a very professional manner. Paying close attention to the specific needs and wants of both the vehicle and the customer.
Every employee has an assigned position and specific tasks that must be performed with each and every vehicle, ensuring the best possible wash experience
