Fund Raising

Fundraising Made Easy!

All it takes is a little effort and salesmanship.

This is how it works
The group will sale Basic "Full Service" Carwash Coupons for $12.95, to all their family, friends, and neighbors. Ultra Touch will donate $6.00 for every Basic wash coupon sold. All Fundraising Groups will be responsible and accountable for all wash coupons issued to them. At the end of the sales period, all Wash Coupons will be inventoried. Any unsold Wash Coupons will return back to Ultra Touch Car Wash. All sold Wash Coupons will be counted and the group will be paid for their efforts and salesmanship!
